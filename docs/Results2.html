4  Results 2

Results of the analysis are shown below. Table 4.1 shows the results of the logistic regressions estimating the likelihood of a track being a song. Table 4.2 shows the results of the same estimate but with the added covariates genre (as a factor variable), tempo, and loudness.

The results of the first model (Table 4.1) indicates that a longer track length is associated with a 2.69 times higher likelihood of being a song. When we add in the other covariates (Model 2, Table 4.2), the likelihood of a track being a song is 1.67 times higher when a track is 1 minute longer.

The results of Models 3 and 4 (Table 4.3 and Table 4.4) show us that track length do not determine the combined liveness and acoustiness of a track. The results for both models were not significant.
