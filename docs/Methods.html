2.2 Methods

[Brooke]

The second portion of our analysis comprises nested logistic regressions. The first model estimates the likelihood that a track is a song with track length as the predictor. The second model estimates the likelihood that a track is both live and acoustic with the same main predictor. Models 3 and 4 mirror models 1 and 2 but include the covariates tempo, loundess, and genre. Results are presented in odds ratios.
